Introduction

Tomoro Yanase (柳瀬友朗) is a Ph.D. student at the Research Section of Severe Storm and Atmospheric Environment, Kyoto University, and also a Junior Research Associate at the Computational Climate Science Research Team, RIKEN R-CCS.

My research interests are Atmospheric Convection and Clouds, and their interplay with the weather and climate system.

The aim of this site is to list and share my works. Note that most contents below are my Evernote pages for easy updating. My CV is here.
